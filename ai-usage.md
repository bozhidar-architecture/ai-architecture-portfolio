This site is built **with AI — deliberately, and under human accountability.** AI-assisted development is a discipline I practise, not one I hide. How I build this portfolio is a small, honest demonstration of how I architect AI systems responsibly.

## How AI is used

I use AI assistants (LLM-based coding and writing tools) to accelerate drafting, restructuring, diagram generation, and infrastructure/config scaffolding, and to pressure-test wording and structure. The **architecture judgement, the decisions, the verification, and the accountability are mine.** AI speeds the work; it does not make the calls.

## The guardrails I apply

The same governance I design into production systems applies to this site:

- **No confidential or proprietary data** ever goes into an AI tool. Anything drawn from real work is **anonymised first** — no internal names, systems, customers, or business logic.
- **Nothing is taken on faith.** Claims are verified against source material; I don't publish AI output I haven't checked. Where something can't be confirmed, it isn't stated.
- **Security by default** — no secrets in prompts or in the repository; automated secret scanning and clean commit history are in place.
- **Human review and accountability** — I read, edit, and stand behind every published word and design decision.

## Why say this at all

Because the medium is the message. I build and govern AI systems for a living, so showing **how** this was made — AI as a force-multiplier under human control, not an unaccountable shortcut — is the point. It's the responsible-AI posture I bring to every system I design.
